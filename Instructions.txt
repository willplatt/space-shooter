To play the game, Blender must be installed; it can be downloaded from http://www.blender.org/download/
To start the game, open "Space shooter game.blend" with Blender and press P while the mouse is within the window.
The spaceship is controlled with the arrow keys and the left mouse button can be clicked or held to shoot.
The game ends when the player has no health remaining or when the player presses the escape key.
